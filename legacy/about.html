Who We Are

Anthony Stipa

Principal, Quickstudy Consulting, LLC

Anthony is academically trained in both broadcast media and journalism, with a degree from Temple University. He began his formal career working in the news division of CBS, identifying stories and writing copy. After a career pivot to PR, he's spent the last 10 years in various capacities, writing and shaping communications strategies for creative agencies, universities, and a city's tourism marketing organization. Today, he spends most of his time cooking up media strategies and campaigns for tech startups around the country.
